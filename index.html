Introduction to AI Learning

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is transforming industries and daily life. In 2025, learning AI is more accessible than ever with abundant online resources, courses, and tools. This page provides a guide to get started or advance your AI skills.

Roadmap to Learning AI

Based on expert advice, here's a step-by-step roadmap:

  1. Start with Basics: Learn Python programming. Use freeCodeCamp Python course or Kaggle tutorials.
  2. Data Handling: Master Pandas and Matplotlib for data manipulation and visualization.
  3. Use AI Tools: Experiment with ChatGPT or similar LLMs to understand concepts.
  4. Build Projects: Create automations and simple models.
  5. Advance to ML/AI: Dive into machine learning algorithms and deep learning.
